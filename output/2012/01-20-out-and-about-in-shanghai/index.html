‘上海！’ - Out And About In Shanghai

This page was written by edk.

Tags: travel, shanghai 2012

Time has passed quickly since I made that quick snip at Hong Kong International Airport, enroute to Shanghai, China.

Every visit I make to China is always packed with food, catching up with friends and family, long nights, long days and plenty of pollution. I found it particularly difficult to climatise to all the crap in the air. But this city just flies... With the population of Australia packed into a metropolis, Shanghai churns 24/7.

^ Angry much? Till next time!
