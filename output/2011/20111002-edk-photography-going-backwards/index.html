EdK Photography Going Backwards...

This page was written by edk.

Tags: edk

Welcome to the new EdK Photography, by Edward Khou. While the site may appear to have gone backwards aesthetically and functionally, I hope this will change in the months ahead as I tweak this site.

Fundamentally, this site should now load faster considering everything in the backend is static (no stored procedures calls to a database, just pure HTML). Bits and bobs are somewhat dynamic but that is all thanks to plugins built with JavaScript for some good old client-side rendering. And the best part about this? Hosted on Amazon's S3.

The overall picture:

  1. Faster browsing experience (I hope so; maybe faster to bounce off my website :D)
  2. Easier for me to maintain the content
  3. More control over the things I want to do
