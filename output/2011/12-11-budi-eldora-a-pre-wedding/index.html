Budi & Eldora - A Pre Wedding

This page was written by edk.

Tags: edk, prewed, lee big stopper

Well, here we are! Sometimes as photographers we put ourselves to shoot only at certain periods of the day. It is why you see an abundance of sunset shots I suppose. This isn't to say otherwise, that sunsets are crap and overdone. No no no. Very frequently 'togs' are thrown into unknown territory. We just need to find our footing and work from there. But here is the thing, it isn't all about us.

Shooting under the scorching Aussie sun always brings challenges along with it in particular for the photographer. But more so for Budi and Eldora. But we persevered and it has paid off! So I just want to send a big thank you to the both of you as well those who were involved! =)

At least we had soaked up as much of the sun we could before all this overcast and ridiculous rain!
